Peaky Blinders

Peaky Blinders made 2013 was created by Steven Knight, is a rough and stylish historical drama set in post World War one Birmingham England. The show follows Thomas Shelby, a aggressive and ambitious gangster, as he goes the dangerous world of crime and power all while expanding his empire and fighting rival gangs. It has Cillian Murphy in a main role, Peaky Blinders has a strong cast, With its sharp writing, compelling characters that perfectly complements the setting.
(4/5 Stars)

Prison Break

Prison Break made in 2005 and created by Paul Scheuring, is a high takes action drama centered on Michael Scofield, a smart structural engineer who makes a plan to break his wrongfully convicted brother, Lincoln Burrows, out of prison. The series follows their escape and the conspiracy behind Lincolns conviction. Great performances by Wentworth Miller and Dominic Purcell, Prison Break keeps viewers on the edge of their seats.
(5/5 Stars)

The Blacklist

The Blacklist made in 2013 and created by Jon Bokenkamp, is a thrilling crime show that has Raymond "Red" Reddington, a brilliant and smart criminal mastermind who surrenders to the FBI with an weird offer: to help capture the worlds most dangerous criminals from his personal "blacklist," but only if he works with the new profiler Elizabeth Keen.
(4/5 Stars)
