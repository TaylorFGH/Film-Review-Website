Oppenheimer

Oppenheimer 2023, directed by Christopher Nolan, is a interesting and thought provoking film drama about J. Robert Oppenheimer, the physicist behind the atomic bomb. The film captures his important role in the Manhattan project during World War 2, showcasing the groundbreaking Trinity test while showing his personal struggles and the ethical problems in his work. With a great performance by Cillian Murphy, with by Emily Blunt and Robert Downey Jr. , the film blends history drama with ambition and responsibility. Nolan's great storytelling and cool visuals make Oppenheimer a great watch.
(5/5 Stars)

Interstellar

Interstellar 2014, directed again by Christopher Nolan, is a visually stunning and emotionally styled scifi that explores humanitys survival in odds of extinction. The film follows Cooper, a former pilot turned farmer, as he leads a space mission to find a new planet while leaving behind his family on a Earth. With sick performances by Matthew McConaughey and Anne Hathaway, Interstellar combines visuals with a human story about love, sacrifice and the passage of time. Nolans direction and Hans Zimmers songs make it an amazing movie.
(5/5 Stars)

Inception

Inception 2010, to no suprise at this point is directed by Christopher Nolan, is a confusing thriller that takes audiences into the world of shared dreaming and manipulation. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within dreams, as he takes on a high stakes mission to implant an idea into a targets mind performing "inception." With a great cast led by Leonardo DiCaprio and supported by Joseph Gordon. Inception blends visuals, an interesting plot, and thoughtful themes about reality and perception. Nolans direction, and again Hans zimmers songs it creates a fantastic film.
(5/5 Stars)
